# Rooting Folder Compatibility Notice

This rooting folder is **only compatible with Android 11 and above**. It is specifically designed to work on devices running ARM-based architectures, including but not limited to **ARM64**, **ARM**, and similar variants.

## Compatibility with Other Systems
While we aim to expand compatibility in the future, particularly for non-Android systems such as Windows, we **strongly advise against attempting to use this rooting folder on Windows** at this time. 

Our efforts to make this rooting folder compatible with Windows are experimental and incomplete. Using it on Windows or other unsupported platforms could result in unexpected behavior, damage to your operating system, or data loss.

## Disclaimer
Please ensure you are using this rooting folder on a compatible Android device to avoid issues. Any modifications or usage outside the intended environment are at your own risk.
